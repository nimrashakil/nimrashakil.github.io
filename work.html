ESA & Pet Wellness

WellnessWag

Turned a 1,145-post blog portfolio with 20 content clusters into a high-performing SEO asset through strategic auditing, content restructuring, and targeted keyword optimization.

Role SEO Content Specialist
Timeframe Oct 2024 - Jan 2025
Vertical ESA, Pet Wellness
254 Positions Improved
11 Positions Declined
77K Monthly Visits
85.84% Non-Branded Traffic

The Challenge

WellnessWag had over 1,100 blog posts across 20 content clusters but lacked a cohesive SEO strategy. Content was being published without keyword targeting, cluster alignment, or performance tracking. Many posts were duplicative, cannibalizing each other's rankings.

The Strategy

I developed a 3-phase content strategy with KPIs, timelines, and weekly action items. This involved a full content audit of all 1,145 posts, merging and deleting duplicate content, and creating a cluster-based optimization roadmap.

  • Analyzed the entire portfolio across 20 clusters and identified content gaps using Semrush Content Gap tool and PostHog analytics
  • Built structured QA workflows evaluating blogs against Semrush readability targets, keyword placement, section depth, and structure
  • Critically evaluated RivalFlow AI competitor gap recommendations, assessing each suggestion for relevance, redundancy, and SEO value
  • Managed on-page SEO, edited AI-generated content, and optimized FAQs across the entire blog portfolio

The Result

254 keyword positions improved against only 11 declined (Oct 2024 to Jan 2026). WellnessWag reached 77K monthly visits with 85.84% non-branded organic traffic, demonstrating that the growth was driven by SEO strategy, not brand recognition.

Weight Loss & Telehealth

Minimal (JoinMinimal)

Drove a 336% increase in site visits for a semaglutide and weight loss telehealth brand through competitive content analysis, content auditing, and strategic SEO recommendations.

Role SEO Content Specialist
Timeframe Oct 2024 - Jan 2025
Vertical Telehealth, Weight Loss
+336% Site Visits
+407% Unique Visitors
100% Non-Branded Traffic
96% Organic Search Channel

The Challenge

Minimal was a newer brand in the competitive semaglutide/weight loss telehealth space, going up against established competitors like Found, Calibrate, Sequence, and Heally. The site had minimal organic presence and needed a content-led growth strategy.

The Strategy

I performed a detailed competitor analysis of Minimal's Semaglutide page against 4 direct competitors, delivering actionable recommendations across clinical evidence, CTAs, content depth, and internal linking.

  • Conducted a full content audit and competitive benchmarking against Found, Calibrate, Sequence, and Heally
  • Identified content gaps using Semrush Content Gap tool and keyword research
  • Delivered recommendations covering clinical evidence integration, CTA optimization, content depth improvements, and internal linking structure
  • Tracked keyword position changes across 68 keywords with measurable improvements

The Result

336% increase in site visits and 407% increase in unique visitors. 96% of traffic came through organic search, and 100% of organic traffic was non-branded, proving the content strategy was capturing entirely new search audiences rather than existing brand awareness.

Cannabis Directory

LeafyMate

Grew organic traffic by 118% for a cannabis dispensary directory through local SEO enhancements, content optimization at scale, and targeted keyword strategies across 3,900+ ranking keywords.

Role SEO Content Specialist
Timeframe Oct 2024 - Jan 2025
Vertical Cannabis, Directory
+118% Organic Traffic Growth
3,900+ Ranking Keywords
100% Non-Branded Traffic
19.1K Monthly Visits

The Challenge

LeafyMate operated as a cannabis dispensary directory with thousands of city and state pages. The site needed local SEO improvements to capture geographically targeted search traffic, and the AI-generated doctor listings required significant cleanup for accuracy and search visibility.

The Strategy

I combined local SEO enhancements with large-scale content editing and on-page optimization to improve the site's search performance across thousands of pages.

  • Added metro areas to city pages to improve local SEO relevance and capture more geographically specific searches
  • Cleaned up, edited, and performed on-page SEO for thousands of AI-generated doctor listings, ensuring accuracy, readability, and search visibility at scale
  • Wrote original SEO-optimized blog posts and resource articles with full keyword research and on-page optimization
  • Managed site audit health scores and tracked keyword position performance across the full portfolio

The Result

118% organic traffic growth with 100% non-branded traffic. The site reached 19.1K monthly visits and ranked for 3,900+ keywords. 99.33% of traffic came from the United States, confirming that the local SEO strategy was hitting the right geographic targets.

B2B SaaS

ChatPandas

Built and executed a full-funnel B2B content strategy for a live chat software company, spanning content strategy, SEO, social media, email marketing, and team leadership over nearly 3 years.

Role Content Strategist
Timeframe Nov 2021 - Aug 2024
Vertical B2B SaaS, Live Chat
+18% Conversion Increase
-15% Bounce Rate Reduction
+25% User Engagement
50K+ LinkedIn Impressions/Quarter

The Challenge

ChatPandas, a subsidiary of LiveChat Ltd, needed a comprehensive content strategy that could drive B2B lead generation across the full marketing funnel while building brand authority in the competitive live chat software market.

The Strategy

I designed a three-tiered content framework aligned to the B2B marketing funnel: TOFU (SEO-driven informational content), MOFU (pain-point targeted whitepapers and webinars), and BOFU (testimonials and case studies). This was supported by a full SEO infrastructure build and a multi-platform social media strategy.

  • Managed a 6-month content production plan: 72 blogs, 18 whitepapers, 6 case studies, 2 research reports, and 2 ebooks organized across three content complexity levels
  • Set up complete SEO infrastructure: Google Search Console, Bing Webmaster Tools, Google Analytics, sitemap, robots.txt, structured data markup, and Core Web Vitals
  • Developed SEO OKRs targeting 30% organic traffic increase, 10-position keyword ranking improvement, 15% CTR increase, and 20% indexed page growth within 6 months
  • Raised LinkedIn impression reach to over 50,000 within a quarter across Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter
  • Led email marketing campaigns that enhanced customer engagement by 10% and reduced email bounce rates by 8%

The Result

18% conversion increase and 15% bounce rate reduction within three months. User engagement improved by 25%, and average page duration increased by 20%. Received top performance ratings (5/5) across all evaluation criteria, reporting directly to the CTO.
